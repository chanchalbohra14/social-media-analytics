What is Social Media Analytics?

Social Media Analytics is the practice of gathering and analyzing data from social media platforms to measure performance, understand audience behavior, and make data-driven decisions. It involves tracking metrics like engagement rates, reach, impressions, and conversions to optimize social media strategies and improve ROI.
